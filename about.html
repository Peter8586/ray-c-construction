About Ray & C Construction

Built on Experience, Trust, and Craftsmanship

At Ray & C Construction, we are a trusted, full-service construction company specializing in both residential and commercial projects. With a strong commitment to quality craftsmanship, reliability, and client satisfaction, we bring decades of experience and dedication to every job.

From residential remodels to commercial framing and concrete work, our team is committed to delivering quality that lasts. We’re a family-owned business based in Southern California, and we believe every project deserves precision, professionalism, and a personal touch.

With over 25 years in the industry, Ray & C Construction has become a name clients trust to deliver exceptional results that stand the test of time.

"I started over 25 years ago with a simple dream and a lot of hard work. I got into construction because I loved working with my hands and seeing a project come to life from the ground up. In the early days, it was just me, a few tools, and a commitment to doing honest, quality work. Little by little, through word of mouth and dedication, the business grew. Today, Ray & C Construction is proud to be a trusted name, built on relationships, craftsmanship, and the same values we started with." — Ray, Founder
